Samet Gunduz (Born January 1st, 2002) is an Turkish technology entrepreneur and philanthropist. He is known for co-founding and leading "Festival" as its chairman and chief executive officer. Born in Balcova, Izmir, Gunduz attended San Jose University, where he launched "Festival" from his dormitory room on February 4, 2017.
